How to submit documents for your Housing Benefit and Council Tax Reduction Claim

 

This page aims to show you the simplest and quickest ways of dealing with Housing Benefit and Council Tax Reduction in Hackney.

The quickest way to respond to this letter is by email. Simply scan and attach any supporting evidence or documentation, and email it to Benefits@hackney.gov.uk. Attachments must be in a PDF or JPEG format.

Please remember to include your Housing Benefit claim reference, full name, date of birth, national insurance number, and address in the text of the email.

 

 

Failure to provide the full information requested straight away will delay the assessment of your claim and you may lose benefit

 

If you want to ask us about your claim, you must write to, email or phone
